## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please email [garethwhitleychard@gmail.com](mailto:garethwhitleychard@gmail.com) rather than opening a public issue. You'll receive a response within 72 hours. Once confirmed, a patch will be released and credited to you (unless you prefer otherwise).
